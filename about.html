About the Author

My name is Terry Franklin and I'm an independent consultant, helping organisations interpret and make sense of their data.

I've previously worked as a software engineer and technical lead for a travel media company that I helped build from scratch over 15 years. During that time I learned a lot about data, the tools to manage it, and how we as humans work best with it.

If you'd like to talk about your organisation's data strategy, I offer a free 20-minute consultation via Skype. Please contact me here to arrange this.
